The Cocktail Economy Is Broken

Creators Don’t Own

Cocktail recipes generate long-term cultural and economic value, yet creators are compensated once and replaced.

Venues Lack Modern Infrastructure

Loyalty programs reward consumption, not community, ownership, or long-term engagement.

Fans Can’t Participate

Guests consume culture but have no stake in the upside they help create.

Our Mission

Crypto Cocktail Club exists to realign cocktail culture so the people who create it, serve it, and support it all participate in its value — transparently, sustainably, and without speculation-first incentives.
